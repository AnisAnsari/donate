    About Us

    Kishor kakade sir, started the initiative with the vision to uplift community by doing charity work of helping all thoes in needs. he has done many Charity work like providing flood relief in kokan, food distribution during lockdown and many many more. His perspective of the world is to be an eutopia of happiness, his ideology is to always strive for spreading happiness along his way of life, this was the objective behind KAAK MAHARAJ JANHIT SEVA SANSTHA

    Our Vision

    To support, handhold, for better life of common man and who are unable to fulfill the basic need of their families, especially farmers and who are backbone of the society.

    Our Mission

    To stop the struggle of survival of an individual and make them free for fulfill their dreams so that they could bright and enhance their life.

    Our Objectives

    Success Stories

    As the Covid- 19 crises continues to emerge over us, the government of India has taken a strict combat measure by directing a lockdown of entire nation. At this great hour of need, the “Kaak Maharaj Janhit Seva Sanstha (KMJSS)” has stepped in to provide relief by providing food to thousands of people across the state and Mumbai.

    Maharashtra is one of the worst affected by the ongoing rains in the region. Nearly 2.5lakh people have already been displaced, making the city cry for help.When nature shows its wrath, no one is speared. Floods in Maharashtra have become an annual phenomenon, displacing thousands in the state every year.“Kaak Maharaj Janhit Seva Sanstha (KMJSS)” was reach to the flood affected communities across Kokan. Truckloads of comprehensive flood relief kits are dispatched. For the people in need, essential material like dry ration etc. have distributed.
